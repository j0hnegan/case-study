Case Study

How Phunware Built a Scalable, Streamlined Content Operation With Kapost

Download As A PDF

The Challenge

Lacking measurable analytics around content production and consumption there was no scalable process to take marketing to the next level. Producing better content was next to impossible when tracking deadlines and deliverables with spreadsheets and Dropbox.

The Solution

Kapost helped Phunware to build best practices into workflows for high level initiatives. The standardized process took out the work of constantly building to-do lists. Now, a repeatable process existed when planning content assets.

“My goals were to organize and streamline our content operation, along with making the right content more accessible to sales. We achieved all of these goals.”

Emily Reynolds, Content Marketing Manager

“My goals were to organize and streamline our content operation, along with making the right content more accessible to sales. We achieved all of these goals.”

Emily Reynolds, Content Marketing Manager

Results

Best-in-Class Content Operation. Emily and her team first used Kapost to develop buyer personas for their five verticals and build workflows accordingly. This was easily accomplished by understanding what to organize and plan when working with the presets that come built into the instance.

Streamlined Communication. Overseeing 80 pieces of content at the same time wouldn’t have been possible without the activity feed. Emily said, “ There is no way we could have that many projects going on at the same time without Kapost. I would be in inbox hell.” The ability to track conversations inside activity feeds pertaining to each content asset or initiative made everything run that much smoother.

Enabling Sales with Content. Using the Kapost Gallery, content is curated using persona tagging, thematic collections, and shareable URLs. The curated view allows the sales team to find the right content, for the right prospect, at the right time.
